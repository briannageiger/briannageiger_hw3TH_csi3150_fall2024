Beaches, Waterfalls, & Glaciers by Bus from Reykjavík

Your Beaches, Waterfalls, & Glaciers adventure starts with a scenic ride on one of our comfortable tour buses. The tour bus departs from our location in Reykjavík located at 411 West First Avenue at 9:45am. After traveling through Reykjavík, you will traverse through the Reykjavík Coastal Wildlife Refuge.

What To Wear

Your trip includes professional guides, raingear, rubber boots, and a small snack. Dress warmly in layers as conditions around the glaciers can be chilly. Please bring thick socks (to wear with provided rubber boots), warm clothes (wear synthetic layers and bring a rain coat), warm hat, and gloves. You may wish to bring binoculars and a camera if you have them.
